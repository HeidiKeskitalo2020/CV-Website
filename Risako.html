Risako 6/2012 - 11/2020

Risako made charming purses and bags from recycled lace betwean 2012 and 2019. Risako had more than 30 resellers in Finland and also one in Germany, Sweden and Japan. I designed and made all the products and of course sell them to te resellers and direct to customers. I also did all the marketing myself: instagram, Facebook, newspaper ads, and other brochures and posters. I also did subcontract work as a design and manufacture for other companies. Later, Risako has works in the beauty industry on a part-time basis, doing color and style analyzes. Now Risako is closed.
